About
I am a graduate from the department of Industrial and Production Engineering in Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology. My undergraduate thesis was in classifying fraudulent nodes in IEEE-CIS dataset with GNN method along with Attention Guided Adaptive Edge Perturbation which I published in the 7th IEOM Conference. My second work is submitted for publication in the 8th IEOM Conference. Two of my recent works - pricing competition under uncertainty using RL agents and forecasting methods comparison - have been submitted and are currently under review.
